What is Atrial Fibrillation?

Atrial fibrillation explained in simple terms

üíì

What Happens?

In atrial fibrillation, the heart's upper chambers (atria) beat irregularly and often too fast. This can lead to an irregular heartbeat and reduced pumping function.

Symptoms

Heart palpitations, shortness of breath, fatigue, dizziness, and chest pain are common symptoms of AFib.

Treatment

Treatment may include medication, cardioversion, ablation, or other procedures, depending on the severity and cause.
