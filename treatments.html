Goals of Treatment

AFib care aims to relieve symptoms, improve quality of life, prevent stroke and heart failure, and reduce hospitalizations. Treatment is personalized based on AFib pattern, duration, comorbidities and patient preference, and often combines multiple approaches.

Indications

PV isolation is effective for symptomatic paroxysmal AF when drug therapy fails or is undesired; persistent AF may require additional substrate ablation. Early referral is increasingly considered, especially in patients seeking rhythm control.
